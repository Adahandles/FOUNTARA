Ways to Earn WTR

  • Provide Storage: Lease your bulk storage tanks to Fountara. We’ll fill them with potable water and pay you in WTR based on gallons stored and duration.
  • Refer Customers: Share your referral code with friends, neighbors and businesses. When they place their first order or reserve Water Bank storage, both you and the new customer receive a WTR bonus.
  • Community Bounties: Join marketing campaigns, content creation, software testing or ecological restoration efforts. Completed tasks are rewarded in WTR.
  • Liquidity Provision: Provide liquidity to WTR trading pairs on decentralized exchanges (DEXes) to earn trading fees and potential farming incentives.
  • Creator Grants: Propose and build tools, apps or integrations that use WTR. Approved proposals receive grants paid in WTR.
