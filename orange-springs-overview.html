Orange Springs Overview

Orange Springs is a historic mineral spring located in northeast Marion County, Florida. Once a famous spa resort, the spring now serves as the source of Fountara’s sustainable bottled water project. The property spans about 67 acres and includes the spring pool, forest and historic structures. With a flow estimated in the millions of gallons per day, Orange Springs is one of Florida’s larger springs. Fountara bottles water from the spring, packaged in recyclable aluminum cans to avoid plastic waste, while preserving the spring’s flow through responsible withdrawal limits and ongoing monitoring.
