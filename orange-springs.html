Mission

The Orange Springs Restoration Project aims to revitalize the historic spring site in Orange Springs, Florida, through sustainable water harvesting and ecological restoration. The project will deliver clean, locally-produced water while restoring natural habitat and supporting the local community.

Phases

Phase 1 – Water Bagging Line: Deploy a water bagging production line to begin packaging Orange Springs water for distribution. This phase is top-priority to create early operational output and revenue.

Phase 2 – Canning Line Expansion: Expand with a canning line funded by Phase 1 profits and WTR token-based fundraising (bond tranches), contingent on Phase 1 success.

Funding & Tokenomics (Overview)

Phase 2 will leverage Fountara’s WTR token model. We plan to offer bond-like token tranches that back equipment and expansion costs. Supporters who participate will help finance infrastructure and may receive rewards tied to water production.
