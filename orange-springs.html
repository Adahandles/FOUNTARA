Project Overview

Preserve and restore a connected freshwater and cultural corridor spanning Seminole Spring, Orange Springs, and the Orange Creek–Orange Lake–Lochloosa system.

Historic ResourceJames W. Townsend House
CountiesMarion, Putnam, Alachua
FocusHydrology, Vegetation, Cultural Protection

Phased Work

  • Phase 1 — Archaeological & burial‑site survey; THPO coordination.
  • Phase 2 — Creek & spring restoration to Rodman, Orange & Lochloosa; DO uplift.
  • Phase 3 — Native re‑vegetation & bio‑engineering shoreline stabilization.
  • Phase 4 — Townsend House stabilization per Secretary Standards.
  • Phase 5 — Conservation buffer & monitoring with quarterly reporting.
