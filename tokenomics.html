Water Tokenomics

The Water Token (WTR) underpins the Fountara ecosystem. Each token represents a claim on our infrastructure and water production capacity. Learn how WTR is distributed, what it funds, and how you can participate.

Supply & Distribution

Total Supply: 50,000 WTR (capped). All tokens are minted at genesis and held by the Fountara treasury.

  • 40% – Reserve & Buyback: Set aside to maintain liquidity, execute buybacks, and underpin the Water Bank reserves.
  • 30% – Development & Infrastructure: Allocated to fund new water wells, treatment facilities, and software development for logistics and token management.
  • 20% – Operations & Expansion: Used to scale our fleet, cover operational expenses, and expand to new regions.
  • 10% – DAO & Community: Reserved for governance, community initiatives, and grants determined by token holders.

These allocations follow the operations model in the attached spreadsheet. Final percentages are subject to audit and community governance.

Emissions & Liquidity Strategy

WTR tokens are emitted gradually rather than all at once. At genesis, 50 000 WTR were minted to the treasury. New WTR enters circulation over a three‑year emissions schedule to fund staking rewards, bond interest and community incentives. Emissions decay by roughly 25 % each year, balancing early growth with long‑term scarcity.

Bond Proceeds to Liquidity: When a bond tranche funds, the ADA raised is paired with treasury WTR and deposited into a liquidity pool on a Cardano DEX (such as Minswap or WingRiders). This provides deep trading liquidity for WTR. LP tokens representing the position are held by the Fountara treasury and locked under a 3‑of‑5 multi‑signature script. Fees earned from the pool accrue back to the treasury to support buybacks and future emissions.

On‑Chain Custody: All treasury assets—including reserves, LP tokens and emission keys—are secured on-chain. A 3‑of‑5 multi‑signature policy (with a mix of core team and community trustees) controls any movement of funds. This ensures that bond proceeds and liquidity positions cannot be misused or withdrawn unilaterally. Transparency dashboards allow anyone to verify the addresses and balances on the Cardano ledger.

Utility & Use Cases

  • Payment: Use WTR to order bulk water, sod, and other products on Fountara.
  • Staking: Stake WTR to earn Water Bank rewards and share in platform fees.
  • Bond Tranches: Acquire bond‑like tranches to fund expansions and earn attractive WTR yields.
  • Governance: Participate in community votes on project roadmaps, tranche terms, and key decisions.
